## Overview
As a developer interested in the intersection of code and design, I use **Blender** to explore technical art and 3D spatial design. What started as an exercise to learn the interface quickly evolved into a deeper study of rendering engines (Eevee and Cycles), procedural materials, and animation timelines. 

This gallery showcases my progression from basic static object modeling to dynamic animations and visual effects (VFX) integration.

---

## 3D Modeling & Environment Design

I focused heavily on understanding lighting setups and material properties, such as the refraction of light through glass or the soft shadows in a low-poly room.
