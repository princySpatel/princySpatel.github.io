## Overview
While many of my projects focus heavily on backend logic and programming, this project was a dedicated exploration of **Level Design and Environment Art**. Built in Unity, the goal was to craft a dense, atmospheric, and fully explorable 3D world from scratch. 

To keep the focus entirely on environmental design and the asset pipeline, I utilized Unity's default Third-Person Robot controller. This allowed me to rapidly test the scale and feel of the world without getting bogged down in custom movement scripts.

## Core World-Building Skills
This tech demo highlights several key engine proficiencies:

* **Custom Terrain Sculpting:** Used Unity's built-in terrain tools to sculpt realistic elevations, valleys, and walkable pathways.
* **Foliage & Asset Painting:** Imported 3D nature assets and utilized terrain brushes to densely populate the world with trees, grass, and bushes, adjusting density and randomness for an organic look.
* **Rapid Prototyping:** Demonstrated the ability to integrate pre-built assets (like the default robot) to quickly test level geometry and environmental scale.
* **Executable Deployment:** Successfully compiled and built the entire environment into a standalone `.exe` for Windows, ensuring the rendering and lighting held up in a finalized build.
